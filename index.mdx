## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick what starts it">
    A price change, a token transfer, a schedule, a webhook, or just a button click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add what it does">
    Send tokens, check balances, post to Slack, swap on Uniswap, or any of 280+ actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wire it together">Each step's output flows into the next, visually on a drag-and-drop canvas.</Step>
  <Step title="Hit publish">B3OS runs it 24/7 and handles the rest.</Step>
</Steps>
